# Visual Identity Brief: Thrust Labs
**Core Concept:** High-Velocity Luxury & Future-Proof Engineering
**Brand Archetype:** The Magician (Transformation through technical mastery)

## 1. Visual Strategy & Mood
The visual language should feel like a high-end aerospace laboratory or a premium electric vehicle interface. It must avoid "corporate blue" and "stock photo" cliches. The goal is to look expensive, fast, and remarkably simple.

## 2. Color Palette (The "Midnight & Neon" Strategy)
* **Primary (The Void):** #0A0A0B (Deep Obsidian). Use this for backgrounds to create a sense of depth and luxury.
* **Secondary (The Pulse):** #00F0FF (Electric Cyan) or #7000FF (Hyper Violet). Used sparingly for "acceleration" accents, glow effects, and call-to-actions.
* **Neutral (The Precision):** #E4E4E4 (Liquid Silver). Used for typography to ensure high legibility against dark backgrounds.
* **Accent (The Status):** #1A1A1A (Matte Carbon). Used for container cards and UI depth.

## 3. Typography Profile
* **Headlines:** A "Technical Sans" (e.g., Michroma, Orbitron, or a customized heavyweight Inter). Geometric, wide tracking, and slightly futuristic.
* **Body Text:** A "High-Precision Sans" (e.g., SF Pro Display or Roboto Mono). Clean, professional, and suggests "code-level" accuracy.
* **Styling:** Heavy use of All-Caps for headers and generous letter spacing (kerning) to evoke a premium feel.

## 4. Logo Conceptualization
* **Iconography:** Abstract representation of upward momentum or "thrust." Think minimal vectors, kinetic lines, or a stylized "T" that doubles as a launch vector.
* **Style:** Flat, monoline, or subtle glassmorphism. No complex gradients; the "future" is found in the geometry, not the decoration.
* **Avoid:** Literal rockets, gears, or old-fashioned "IT" globes.

## 5. Imagery & Art Direction
* **Style:** Dark mode UI/UX, long-exposure light trails, and macro shots of high-end hardware (circuitry, brushed metal).
* **Lighting:** High contrast (Chiaroscuro) with neon "rim lighting" to suggest energy moving through a dark space.
* **Composition:** Minimalist, centered, and symmetrical to convey "Trust" and "Strategy."

## 6. Design Principles for AI Generation
* "Create a minimalist B2B website landing page for a luxury software consultancy called Thrust Labs. Dark mode, obsidian backgrounds with electric cyan accents. 8k resolution, cinematic lighting, sleek, futuristic, carbon fiber textures, high-fidelity UI."
* "Logo design for 'Thrust Labs', minimalist vector, geometric abstraction of speed and precision, high-end tech aesthetic, matte black and silver."

## Implementation Notes
- Font stack: Orbitron (display headlines) + Space Grotesk (body/UI) + Space Mono (stats/numbers)
- Primary accent: #00F0FF with `box-shadow: 0 0 24px rgba(0,240,255,0.35)` for glow
- Secondary accent: #7000FF for depth/hover states
- All sections use obsidian/carbon backgrounds — no light-mode sections
- CTA buttons: solid cyan bg with glow shadow, near-black text for contrast
- Cards: #1A1A1A with 1px border rgba(255,255,255,0.08) and subtle inner refraction border
